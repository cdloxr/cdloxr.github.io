App Wireframe Project: Home Cooked Meals!

For one of my projects, I worked in a team to design a wireframe for an app that help encourage users to cook at home by promoting nutritional health and providing recipes.

Description: The app allows users to search up recipes with their desired macronutrient content. The app would then show recipes that match with the criteria. Users could create an account to save their favorite recipes.

Tool Used: Figma

Research Paper: YouTube and Copyright!

In one of my classes, we dove into the internet and cyberlaw where we reviewed cases that structure the laws around the internet today like copyright and fair use. For our final project I wrote a research paper about the relationship of YouTube content creators and Copyright law, addressing YouTube's copyright system Content ID.

  • Title: The Impact of Copyright Policies on Youtube Creators
  • Key Topics: Fair Use, DMCA Takedowns, Monetization Issues

Take a look into the world of YouTube Content creators and the complexities of copyright law and fair use.
